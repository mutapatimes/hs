WooCommerce store

Shopify, BigCommerce, WhatsApp Web and Gmail work out of the box. For WooCommerce, add your store's address so the badge can run inside your own wp-admin. You'll be asked to grant access to that one site.

    How to use it

    1. Open a customer or order in your store admin, a chat in WhatsApp Web, or an email in Gmail.
    2. The Halia card appears in the corner with their grade, why, and the next move.
    3. Pick a message template and copy it, or jump to the client in Halia.
