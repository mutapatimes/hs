Privacy Policy

Halia is built so that we never hold your customers. This policy explains what we process, the little we store, and how we keep it safe.

The short version. When a merchant connects their store, Halia reads their customers, scores them in memory, and discards them within minutes. We do not copy your customer database onto our servers. The only thing we store is a merchant's own account details and an encrypted, read-only key.

1. Who we are

Halia ("we", "us") provides hidden-VIC scoring software for retailers. For a merchant's own customer data, the merchant is the data controller and Halia acts as a data processor, processing that data only to provide the service and only on the merchant's instructions.

2. Customer data we process for merchants (and do not store)

To score a merchant's customers we read, in memory only, fields such as name, email, phone, billing and shipping address, order totals and counts, and payment method. This data is processed transiently to produce a score and is not written to any Halia database. It exists in memory for the duration of a scoring run, typically a few minutes, and is then discarded.

3. Account data we do store

  • The merchant's store URL and an encrypted, read-only API key (or platform token).
  • Merchant settings: VIC threshold, latent-value benchmarks, email templates, sign-off name.
  • A one-way hash of the private dashboard-link token used to authenticate access.
  • Basic contact details a merchant gives us, such as a work email.

This is not customer personal data. Secrets are encrypted at rest.

4. How we use data

To provide, secure, and support the service, to authenticate access to a dashboard, and to communicate with merchants about their account. We do not sell data, and we do not use a merchant's customer data to train models or for advertising.

5. Legal bases

For merchant account data, performance of our contract and our legitimate interest in running the service. For customer data processed on a merchant's behalf, the merchant determines the legal basis as controller, and we act under a processor agreement.

6. Sharing and subprocessors

We use a small number of infrastructure providers to run Halia, including our cloud hosting provider. We push scores and segments only to the tools a merchant explicitly connects, such as their marketing platform. We do not share data with anyone else except where required by law.

7. Retention

Customer data: not retained, as described above. Merchant account data and secrets: kept while an account is active and deleted on disconnection or request, subject to any legal retention duties.

8. International transfers

Where data is processed outside your region, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as standard contractual clauses. Because we do not store customer data, the practical transfer footprint is limited to account data.

9. Your rights

Individuals can exercise data-subject rights (access, correction, erasure, and others) through the merchant who controls their data. As there is no customer data stored with Halia, erasure on our side is satisfied by design. Merchants can contact us to exercise rights over their account data.

10. Cookies

Our application uses a single essential cookie to keep you signed in. See our Cookie Policy.

11. Contact

Questions or requests: hello@halia.app.
