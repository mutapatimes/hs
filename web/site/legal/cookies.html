Cookie Policy

Halia uses as few cookies as possible. We do not run advertising or cross-site tracking cookies in the application.

Essential cookies

The Halia dashboard sets one essential cookie, halia_t, which holds your private access token so you stay signed in as you move between pages. It is strictly necessary to provide the service and cannot be switched off without losing access. It is not used for tracking.

This marketing site

Our public marketing pages load a web font from Google Fonts to render type. No analytics or advertising cookies are set on these pages by default. If we add optional analytics in future, we will ask for your consent first.

Managing cookies

You can clear or block cookies in your browser settings. Blocking the essential cookie will sign you out of the dashboard.
