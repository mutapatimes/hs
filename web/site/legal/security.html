Security overview

Security & compliance

Halia is built zero-retention from the first line of code. The strongest way to keep your customers' data safe is to never hold it, so we do not.

Zero-retention architecture

When Halia scores a store, it reads customers in real time, scores them in memory, and discards them within minutes. Customer personal data is never written to a Halia database. There is no standing copy of your customer base on our side to leak, lose, or be compelled to hand over.

Read-only access

Halia connects to your store with read-only credentials. We can read orders and customers to score them. We cannot change, delete, refund, or place anything. You can revoke the key at any time and access ends immediately.

Encryption

The single secret we store, your read-only key or token, is encrypted at rest. All connections to your store and to the tools you connect run over encrypted, authenticated channels.

What we store, and what we do not

  • Stored: store URL, an encrypted read-only key, your settings, a hashed access-link token, and contact details.
  • Not stored: your customers' names, emails, addresses, orders, or any personal data derived from them.

Data protection and GDPR

For your customer data, you are the controller and Halia is your processor, acting on your instructions. Because we retain no customer data, data-subject access and erasure requests are satisfied by design on our side. We can provide a data processing agreement on request.

Hosting and subprocessors

Halia runs on a reputable cloud platform. We keep the list of subprocessors short and will share it on request, along with their security posture.

Responsible disclosure

If you believe you have found a vulnerability, please email hello@halia.app with details. We will acknowledge and work with you to resolve it, and we will not pursue good-faith research that respects our users' privacy.
