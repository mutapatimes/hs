Security & data

Built so we never hold your customers.

The safest way to protect your customers' data is to never hold it. So we don't. Halia looks at your customers only for the moment it takes to score them, and keeps no copy. There is no customer list sitting on our side to leak, lose, or be forced to hand over.

Zero-retention architecture

Your data flows through Halia. It never settles in it.

Read-only by design

We can look. We cannot touch.

Halia connects to your store with read-only credentials. We can read orders and customers in order to score them. We cannot change, delete, refund, or place anything. The single secret we hold is that read-only key, encrypted at rest. Revoke it and Halia goes dark in the same moment.

What we keep, and what we never do

A short list, by design.

+What Halia stores

  • Your store URL
  • One encrypted, read-only key
  • Your settings: thresholds, templates, sign-off
  • A one-way hash of your access link
  • A contact email you give us

−What Halia never stores

  • Your customers' names
  • Their email addresses or phone numbers
  • Their billing or delivery addresses
  • Their orders or payment details
  • Any score or profile derived from the above
Scored in memory

Here for minutes, then gone.

When Halia scores your store, customers are processed in memory and never written to a Halia database. A scoring run lasts minutes, and when it ends the data is gone. We never share your data, never pool it with other brands, and never train on it or reuse it as reference for anyone else. Because nothing of your customers lives with us, data-subject access and erasure requests are satisfied on our side by design, not by a manual process.

The rest of the posture
01

Encryption everywhere

Secrets encrypted at rest. Every call to your store and your tools runs over encrypted, authenticated channels.

02

You are the controller

For your customer data you are the controller and Halia is your processor, acting only on your instructions.

03

Instant revocation

Disconnect or revoke the key and our access ends immediately. There is nothing left of your customers to delete.

04

Audited infrastructure

Halia runs on Render, which is SOC 2 Type 2 and ISO 27001 certified and independently penetration-tested every year. We keep our subprocessor list short and share it on request.

05

Keys held apart

The one secret we keep, your read-only store key, is encrypted, and the key that decrypts it is held separately from the database, so a database dump alone can never unlock it.

06

Rate-limited & monitored

Sign-in and scoring endpoints are rate-limited against abuse, our dependencies are monitored for known vulnerabilities, and customer data is never written to our logs.

07

GDPR-aligned by design

Because no standing customer data lives with us, data-subject access, portability, and erasure are largely answered by the architecture itself. We provide a DPA and profiling wording for your own privacy notice.

08

Responsible disclosure

Found something? Email us. We will acknowledge, work with you, and never pursue good-faith research.
