Security & data

Your customer list
stays yours.

Most tools copy your whole customer list onto their servers and keep it there. Halia works the other way: it reads your customers for the moment it takes to score them, then releases the data. The access it needs is read-only, and yours to switch off whenever you like.

Read our security overview →

01Read-only

Halia can read your store to score it. It can never change, delete, or place an order.

02Nothing stored

Your customers are scored in memory and discarded within minutes. There is no database of them on our side to breach.

03You control access

The one permission Halia holds is to look, and you can revoke it in a click. Access ends at once.

04Encrypted in transit

Every connection to your store and your tools is encrypted, so nothing can be read along the way.
