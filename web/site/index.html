Security & data

Built so we never
hold your customers.

Most tools copy your customer database onto their servers. Halia does the opposite. Your customers are read in real time, scored in memory, and discarded within minutes. The only thing we ever keep is one read-only key, encrypted at rest. Revoke it and Halia goes dark instantly. Because there is no customer data on our side, data-subject and erasure requests are satisfied by design.

Read our security overview →

01Read-only access

A read-only connection to your store. Halia can never change, delete, or place an order.

02Scored in memory

Customers are processed in RAM and never written to a Halia database. Minutes later, gone.

03Encrypted, revocable key

The single secret we store is encrypted at rest. Pull the key and access ends at once.

04GDPR by design

Nothing of your customers lives with us to return, export, or delete. Compliance, not paperwork.

05You keep every record

Your data stays in your store and your tools. Halia is the lens, never the vault.

06Encryption in transit

Every call to your store and your tools runs over encrypted, authenticated connections.
