Docs/Step 2

Connect your email & CRM.

This step is optional, Halia works on its own. But if you connect the email or CRM tool you already use, each customer's grade shows up right there, so your VICs are easy to find and email. And Halia never emails your customers itself; you always choose what to send.

How this works

Connecting a tool lets Halia write each customer's grade into it, using a key you provide. You stay in control, and the same promise holds: Halia never keeps a copy of your customers.

Connect as many as you like from your dashboard settings, or skip this and work straight from the Halia dashboard. Pick a tool below.

Klaviyo

KKlaviyoOptional

Halia adds each customer's grade to their Klaviyo profile, and can build a list of any group you pick, ready to email or start a flow.

  1. Find your API key

    In Klaviyo, go to Settings → API keys and create a private key.

  2. Paste it into Halia

    In your Halia dashboard, open Integrations → Klaviyo and paste the key.

  3. You're connected

    Each customer now carries a Halia grade in Klaviyo, and you can build a list from any selection.

Mailchimp

MMailchimpOptional

Halia labels your existing contacts with their grade, so you can filter for your VICs and email them.

  1. Find your API key

    In Mailchimp, go to Account → Extras → API keys and create one.

  2. Connect and pick your audience

    Paste the key into Integrations → Mailchimp in Halia, and choose which audience to enrich.

  3. You're connected

    Contacts get a grade tag like Halia A* you can filter and send to.

No one gets subscribed. Halia only tags people already on your list, it never opts anyone into marketing or changes their consent.

HubSpot

HHubSpotOptional

Halia adds a grade and score to your HubSpot contacts, and can build a list of a group you choose.

  1. Create a private app

    In HubSpot, go to Settings → Integrations → Private Apps and create one called “Halia”.

  2. Let it read and update contacts

    Give the app permission to read and update contacts, then copy the token it shows you.

  3. Paste it into Halia

    Open Integrations → HubSpot in Halia and paste the token. Each contact now shows its Halia grade.

Slack

SSlackOptional

Get a message in Slack the instant a VIC places an order, so your team can send a thank-you while it still feels personal.

  1. Add a Slack webhook

    In Slack, add the Incoming Webhooks app and choose a channel (for example #vic-alerts).

  2. Paste the link into Halia

    Copy the link Slack gives you into Integrations → Slack.

  3. You're connected

    Halia posts the customer, their grade, and why, the moment they order.

Reaching out

When Halia spots a VIC, it can draft a warm message for you, from a few ready-made templates or a personal note.

You read it over and hit send, from your own inbox, from WhatsApp, or from your email tool. Halia never sends for you, you're always the one who chooses. See Using Halia for how outreach works day to day.
