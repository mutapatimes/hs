The refund guarantee

The scan finds them, or it refunds itself.

If a paid onboarding scan surfaces fewer qualifying VICs than your tier promises (10 for Discovery, 20 for Signal and Atelier), the scan fee is fully refundable. And “a VIC” has a precise definition: a customer who fires at least one wealth or intent signal and sits below your own VIP spend threshold, exactly the definition your dashboard counts. The monthly subscription is separate and month-to-month; disconnect any time.
